Hi, I'm Hiro.

Over the last three years, I have been working on a long-term project about the mathematics of one-cut origami. I started from learning the theory behind “one complete straight cut”, then moved on to designing crease patterns, and finally built my own program to generate them automatically. This site is a place to collect those studies and experiments.
